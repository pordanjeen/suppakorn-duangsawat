Ninja 300 – Sport Bike Classification

Street Sport • Track Capable

The Kawasaki Ninja 300 is classified as a sport motorcycle, not a pure race bike. It is designed primarily for street riding while offering performance features that make it suitable for track use and beginner-level racing.

  • Bike Type: Sport Bike
  • Race Ready: No (Street Legal)
  • Track Use: Yes (Track Days & Training)
  • Riding Position: Sporty yet Comfortable
  • Purpose: Daily Riding, Touring, Track Practice
